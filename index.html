What it does

Built for appointment-based clinics: answer common questions, guide patients to book, and capture callback requests.

Answer routine questions

  • Opening hours, location, services
  • Insurance and pricing ranges (only clinic-approved)
  • Reduces front-desk load and missed calls

Drive bookings

  • Direct "Book appointment" link
  • Clear handoff for medical questions
  • Emergency detection and escalation

Capture leads

  • "Call me back" requests
  • Stores messages securely for clinic staff
  • Works on mobile & desktop

Built for healthcare safety

The assistant is designed to provide general information and route patients correctly - not replace clinical judgment.

No medical advice

  • No diagnoses or treatment recommendations
  • For symptoms/pain: directs to booking or contact
  • Clear disclaimers in the chat UI

Emergency handling

  • Detects emergency signals
  • Shows clinic emergency instructions
  • Encourages urgent care when needed

Language-aware

  • Responds in the user's language
  • Clinic-configured supported languages
  • Simple, friendly patient experience
